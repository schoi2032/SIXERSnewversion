What's a Boom Trip?

The Boom Trip is an exciting event that all sixth graders participate in.
The goal of the trip is to help students build stronger connections with one another while having fun.

It’s a two-day experience — the first day takes place at CI, and the second day is held at Wavepark.
Students can choose between two thrilling activities: scuba diving or surfing,

making it an unforgettable adventure for everyone.
