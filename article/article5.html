STUCO Events: Upcoming Festivities

Chadwick International Student Council has hosted an entertaining event for MS students. The event encompassed a music jeopardy quiz, a try-not-to-laugh contest, along with ramen eating contest. The music jeopardy quiz consisted of various genres of K-pop, pop, jazz, etc. With K-pop quizzes with the loudest reactions and the highest accuracy, the winner became grade 6 and 7. The try not to laugh contest contains jokes, some that broke up laughter, and some awkward ones. With an outstanding performance of Suveer from grade 6, the contest concluded. The ramen-eating contest receives the largest ovation from the students. With fierce competition and cheering, Grade 7 students had earned the triumph by eating 6 ramen in 15 minutes.
