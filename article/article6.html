Newsies, CI MS production

Starting October, a group of students from 6th, 7th, and 8th grade came together to practice for a wonderful play, Newsies Jr.. It has been a tradition to do a production every year at CI. The Upper School has also performed The Tempest a while ago, and now it is Middle School’s turn! They have worked hard for it and have been practicing every week at rehearsals and also at weekends for bootcamps. The director is the Middle School’s own theater teacher, Mr. Wenny. Mark your calendars, CIMS is watching Newsies Jr. on Thursday, February 26 during advisory time through block 2! Break a leg!
