Songdo Cup- High Expectations for CI

The Songdo Cup is one of the most anticipated annual soccer tournaments hosted by Chadwick International in Songdo, Incheon. Each year, schools from across Korea come together to compete for the prestigious trophy, showcasing their teamwork, sportsmanship, and passion for the game. This year’s competition is set to be bigger and more exciting than ever — with teams not only from Korea but also from the United States and China joining the event for the first time, making it an international celebration of youth soccer. Last year, the current Grade 6 Boys & Girls teams brought pride to Chadwick International by winning the Songdo Cup, setting a high bar for this year’s competitors. With their impressive performance and strong team spirit, the pressure — and excitement — are on to see if they can defend their title against new challengers. The tournament promises thrilling matches, unforgettable moments, and a great opportunity for players and fans alike to come together and share their love for soccer.
