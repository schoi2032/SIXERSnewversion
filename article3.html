Grade 6 Boys and Girls Win Medals in Songdo Cup

Chadwick International's Grade 6 boys took the gold medal in the Songdo Cup on Nov. 2, after beating SFS in the final 2-0. Grade 6 Girls beat Koram FC in the Semifinals before an unlucky loss to SFS in the finals 3-0. In the boys match, Hugh Lee scored a goal in the first 5 minutes before SFS could react. Then, after an unfortunate Penalty Kick miss by Chadwick, they scored again- after a free kick by Jay Koo that was hit in by Allen Kim. Grade 6 girls had a few chances to score, but there were unlucky situations and they unfortubately lost 3-0. This encouranged both teams to keep their love for soccer and try hard next year too.
