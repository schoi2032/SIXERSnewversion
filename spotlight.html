Celebrating the achievements and personalities of our wonderful Sixers

Edward Lee

Grade 6 Student

Favorite Subject: PHE, Sports

Hobbies: Boxing, Basketball

Dream Job: Basketball or Boxing Athlete

Fun Fact: Edward usually plays basketball, but he is better at boxing!

Edward recently made his mark at the 1st Yongsan District Boxing Tournament, where he fought his way to the finals and came out on top. Representing Grade 6, he showed incredible focus, strength, and determination in every round. His victory wasn't just about winning a title- it was about proving his hard work and dedication in the ring. Edward's triumph in the tournament stands as a powerful start to what looks like a promising boxing journey ahead.

Grade 6 Student

Favorite Sport: N/A, Prefers Music

Talents: Playing the Cello

Family: DJ is an only child!

It wasn't easy-there were nerves, long rehearsals, and tough competition-but his passion carried him through. In the end, he earned a certificate for his performance, a proud reminder of how far he's come. More than the award, it was the experience itself that stuck with him-the music, the people, and the feeling of doing what he loves on a big stage.
